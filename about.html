ABOUT THE PROJECT

A browser for the observed Universe.

NĀSADĪYA LIGHTCONE is an open, survey-native visualisation environment. It separates measured structure from reconstruction: sky coverage, selection functions, redshift precision and source provenance remain part of the view.

Research principle

The project treats a survey visualisation as an instrument-facing interface. A visible gap can be a mask. A radial coordinate can carry uncertainty. A dense region may reflect selection as well as structure. These conditions are communicated rather than hidden.

Built by Biswajit Jana

Biswajit Jana is an astrophysics researcher with an engineering background. NĀSADĪYA connects observational cosmology, scientific communication and browser-native visualisation.

It is released for public exploration, transparent methods and future community contribution.

What it is not

NOT A SIMULATIONNo galaxies are fabricated to fill catalogue gaps and no cosmic-web filaments are reconstructed for display.
NOT A SINGLE CATALOGUE CLAIMEach layer retains its own scope, footprint and measurement type.
NOT A BLACK BOXCatalogue transforms, tile-store design and source ledgers are documented in the public repository.
